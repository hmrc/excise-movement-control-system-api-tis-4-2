## C222
```
IF <EXPORT DECLARATION ACCEPTANCE/RELEASE. Export Declaration Acceptance or Goods Released for Export > is "0"
THEN
<EXPORT DECLARATION ACCEPTANCE/RELEASE.Date of Acceptance> is 'R'
<EXPORT DECLARATION ACCEPTANCE/RELEASE.Date of Release> does not apply
ELSE
<EXPORT DECLARATION ACCEPTANCE/RELEASE.Date of Acceptance> does not apply
<EXPORT DECLARATION ACCEPTANCE/RELEASE.Date of Release> is 'R'
```
